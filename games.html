Professional Projects

Sweetlads' Werewolf

Sweetlads’ Werewolf is a casual take on the classic party game of deduction and deception, and they needed someone to take on the programming for the backend systems. As part of the team, I expanded the functionality of online play, and took on the responsibility for managing the game’s servers.

Gumshoe

Triple Eye Games needed someone to develop a working tech demo for their film noir themed narrative roguelike. I took on the task and acted as the sole developer for the project’s lifespan, working on all aspects of gameplay and a datapack-driven system designed to be both flexible and modular. The final game allows players to pick a narrative style for their character, and presents a story made up reactive encounters that change with each playthrough.

Outland Odyssey

Outland Odyssey sought to distinguish itself through the development of unique procedural level generation. I was tasked with the design, production, and maintenance of this system. The result enabled the design team to produce quality content in a far higher volume than was previously possible.

Viking Land

I was brought on to take charge of the frontend codebase. I took ownership of existing mechanics and created new systems according to design requirements and player feedback leading to increased player retention and positive reviews.

WURD

I joined to support the existing devops team. After establishing the client’s needs and requirements, my duties included building a framework that was compatible with the existing software systems. The final product became a key retention point for a portion of the userbase in their interactions with the parent application.

Personal Projects and Game Jams

Banding

My entry for the 2023 Rainbow Game Jam with the theme “Coming Together”. Putting diverse characters together makes the team stronger, and careful positioning allows characters to take hits or heal in order to overcome tougher monsters.

Earth Remembers

Earth Remembers is a digital roleplaying experience made for the 2019 Bonn climate summit. I served as the lead designer and the secondary programmer.

Reality Shift

A top-down 2D action adventure game that focuses on the player’s ability to move between two versions of the same world at will.

Monumentous

Players start with a few basic building cards and after placing every building, they are offered a choice of a new one and an action card to add to their deck.
